Digital Recreation

My favorite part of designing comes from the learning aspects. I love how some designs can be done though multiple different mediums and perspectives to entice different reactions from an audience.

This is a recreation that I did for a customer due to the original being unavailable. I scanned in the card (left) and used Illustrator to trace over the head and then created the antlers with the brush and pen tool. The final card design was done in copper thermography.
